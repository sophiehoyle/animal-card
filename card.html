Arctic Fox

The Arctic fox is a small fox native to the Arctic regions of the Northern Hemisphere and common throughout the Arctic tundra biome.

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes lagopus
  • Average Length: 80 - 110cm
  • Average Lifespan: 3 - 4 years in the wild
  • Habitat:Arctic regions

The Arctic Fox is well adapted to living in cold environments, and is best known for its thick, warm fur that is also used as camouflage.
